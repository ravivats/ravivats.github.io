I worked at Incubation and Consultancy Unit (ICUCS), Ramaiah Institute of Technology as a Mobile and Web Application Developer during my college days.

I worked on a project called 'Tally Reports', which deals with automatically fetching a company's accounts data from Tally ERP on any change made to it and displaying it to the user, who is the owner of the company, on a Mobile App.

Areas that I worked on were:
1. Report Retrieval from Tally ERP
2. Data retrieval from the server
3. UI/UX, populating Charts, ListViews and CardViews of the "Tally Report Genie" Mobile App
